The Style Atlas

Named style families you can point an AI at. Read a family in three questions: substrate, hierarchy carrier, edge signature. Ten families here: five light (Lesson 04), five dark and bold (Lesson 05). The two Brutalisms get their own deep-dive in Lesson 06.

Light-mode families (Lesson 04)

01 · Premium Utilitarian Minimalism

Smells like: Notion, Craft, Linear docs. A document pretending to be an app.

Substrate
Pure white #FFFFFF or warm bone #F7F6F3
Hierarchy
Editorial serif display (Lyon, Newsreader) over a clean sans (SF Pro, Geist, Switzer)
Edges
Exactly 1px solid #EAEAEA; radius 4-12px; shadows near zero (< 0.05 opacity)
Signature
Faux-OS window chrome, <kbd> keys, washed-out pastel tags, ink text #2F3437, gray #787774
Use when
Productivity tools, docs-like products, calm SaaS that must read as trustworthy and quiet

Defined by minimalist-ui (upstream: minimalist-skill)

02 · Editorial Luxury

Smells like: Kinfolk magazine, boutique hotels, high-end real estate, agencies.

Substrate
Warm cream #FDFBF7 with a subtle noise/film-grain overlay (~3-5% opacity)
Hierarchy
Massive high-contrast variable serif headings, tight tracking and line-height
Edges
Barely any: rules and whitespace, not boxes
Signature
Muted sage or espresso accents, small tracked caps for meta text, whitespace treated as the product
Use when
Lifestyle, real estate, print-adjacent brands, anywhere "considered" beats "efficient"

Defined by high-end-visual-design §3.A.2 (upstream: soft-skill)

03 · Soft Structuralism

Smells like: Apple Health, premium consumer wellness, polished portfolios.

Substrate
Silver-grey #F3F4F6 or plain white; cool, airy
Hierarchy
Massive bold grotesk (800 weight, tight tracking)
Edges
No borders. White components float on ultra-diffuse ambient shadows; radius 16-20px
Signature
Stat cards with big numerals, pill CTAs, everything breathing
Use when
Consumer health, personal metrics, friendly-premium products for a broad audience

Defined by high-end-visual-design §3.A.3 (upstream: soft-skill)

04 · Pristine Light

Smells like: quality print, Stripe's editorial pages, confident documentation.

Substrate
Off-white paper #FAF7F2, no texture tricks
Hierarchy
Sharp near-black sans with a strong size scale; numbered index rows
Edges
Hairline rules instead of cards; radius ~0-2px; no shadows
Signature
Reads like a well-set book page: editorial confidence, zero ornament
Use when
Content-first pages, methods, manifestos, pricing that wants to feel honest

Defined by imagegen-frontend-web §2, Theme Paradigm 1

05 · Quiet Premium Neutral

Smells like: Aesop, premium skincare, boutique spas, restrained luxury goods.

Substrate
Bone, sand, taupe, stone: #E9E4DB family, never white
Hierarchy
Light-to-regular weights, wide-tracked uppercase wordmarks, small sizes
Edges
Near-square (radius 2-3px), thin single-color borders on CTAs
Signature
Contrast lowered on purpose; the restraint is the brand
Use when
Premium goods where shouting would cheapen; note the a11y cost of low contrast, check body text still passes

Defined by imagegen-frontend-web §2, Theme Paradigm 4

Dark and bold families (Lesson 05)

06 · Ethereal Glass

Smells like: AI startups, Linear, Vercel, Arc, dark SaaS.

Substrate
Deepest OLED black #050505 with dim colored orbs (emerald, blue) as a radial mesh
Hierarchy
Wide geometric grotesk, near-white on black
Edges
Frosted cards: backdrop-filter: blur() + 1px white/14 border + inner highlight (inset 0 1px 0 rgba(255,255,255,.2)) for a refraction rim; radius 16px
Signature
You can see through the cards. Ship a solid-fill fallback for prefers-reduced-transparency
Use when
AI and dev tools, premium tech, Apple-adjacent product launches

Defined by high-end-visual-design §3.A.1; glass rim from design-taste-frontend §5

07 · Deep Dark Mode

Smells like: GitHub dark, Raycast, Vercel dashboard, most product dark modes.

Substrate
Graphite / zinc #0E0E11, deliberately not pure black
Hierarchy
Bold grotesk, zinc-white text; one restrained accent used sparingly
Edges
Solid cards #17171C with a 1px #26262E border; radius 12px; no blur, no frost
Signature
The workhorse product dark mode. Elegant glow only when justified
Use when
Dashboards, dev products, anything that needs a calm, legible dark UI

Defined by imagegen-frontend-web §2, Theme Paradigm 2

08 · Dark Tech / Hacker

Smells like: devtools, CLIs, security products, terminal-first brands.

Substrate
Near-black, faintly green #0A0F0A
Hierarchy
Monospace only; neon green #4ADE80 accent
Edges
Terminal window chrome, thin dim-green borders, blocky
Signature
Terminal motifs: a prompt you type, a blinking cursor, command output. You type here
Use when
Developer tools, CLIs, security, anything that wants engineer credibility

Defined by design-taste-frontend §2.B (dark tech / hacker)

09 · Tactical Telemetry

Smells like: aerospace HUDs, trading terminals, mission control, sci-fi interfaces.

Substrate
Deep black #06080A
Hierarchy
Monospace, amber phosphor #FFB000 with a faint glow; tabular numerals
Edges
ASCII framing: corner brackets, crosshair ticks, dashed dividers
Signature
Instrument readouts: coordinates, status blocks, high-density data. You monitor here
Use when
Data-heavy monitoring, ops, anything that wants a mission-control feel

Defined by industrial-brutalist-ui §2.2 (deep-dive in Lesson 06)

10 · Bold Studio Solid

Smells like: Gumroad, Linktree, bold DTC and creator brands.

Substrate
One saturated color field edge to edge: royal blue, oxblood, forest, vermilion, or emerald
Hierarchy
Heavy grotesk, crisp high-contrast near-white type on the color
Edges
Sharp, radius 4px; a single bright contrasting pop (e.g. yellow) reserved for the CTA
Signature
The only bold family that is not dark. Confidence through one committed color
Use when
Creator tools, DTC, launches, anywhere a brand wants to feel loud and sure

Defined by imagegen-frontend-web §2, Theme Paradigm 3

Telling the lookalikes apart

PUM vs Pristine
PUM is an app (chrome, kbd keys, bordered blocks, pastel tags). Pristine is a page (rules, no containers, print energy).
Pristine vs Luxury
Both on paper tones. Luxury leads with a massive serif and whitespace; Pristine leads with a sharp sans and clarity.
Quiet vs the rest
Background turns stone or sand and contrast drops on purpose: you are in Quiet Neutral.
Glass vs Deep Dark
Glass is frosted (you see through cards) on OLED black with orbs. Deep Dark is solid graphite cards, no blur.
Hacker vs Telemetry
Both mono neon-on-black. Hacker is a terminal you type in; Telemetry is an instrument you monitor.
Studio vs all
Only Studio Solid drops black and white entirely for one confident saturated field.
