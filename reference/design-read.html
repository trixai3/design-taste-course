The Design Read

The one sentence to say before touching anything. The audience picks the aesthetic, not your taste. Source: design-taste-frontend §0, §2.

The formula

Reading this as: [page kind] for [audience],
with a [vibe] language,
leaning toward [foundation].

The four parts

1 · Page kind

Landing, portfolio, service page, editorial, dashboard. Sets the ground rules.

2 · Audience

Who scans this and what they need. This is what actually sets the dials.

3 · Vibe

Two or three words: trust-first, premium editorial, playful, brutalist, accessibility-first.

4 · Foundation

A real design system when the brief calls for one, or an honest aesthetic family with a named typeface.

Brief → foundation map

Indie / small-team AI marketingTailwind v4Default for indie builds.
Agency, editorial, brutalist, premiumnative CSSNo official package. Build with a named typeface, honestly labeled.

Honesty rule: if the brief matches a real system, install the official package. Do not recreate its CSS by hand, and do not call an aesthetic trend (glassmorphism, bento) an official system.
