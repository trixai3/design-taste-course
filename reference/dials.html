The Three Dials

Every page reads as a triple: Variance / Motion / Density, each 1-10. Set them from the design read, not by habit. Baseline is 8 / 6 / 4. Source: design-taste-frontend §1, §7.

Variance · 变化度 — symmetry to chaos

1–3 · low

Predictable

Centered, symmetric, equal padding, 12-col balance. Trust-first, public-sector.

4–7 · mid

Offset

Split heroes, left-aligned headers, varied aspect ratios, some overlap.

8–10 · high

Asymmetric

Broken grid, unequal fr-units, masonry, large deliberate empty zones.

Motion · 动效强度 — static to cinematic

1–3 · low

Static

Hover and active states only. No automatic animation. The reduced-motion default.

4–7 · mid

Fluid

Transitions, entrance fades, staggered reveals. Transform and opacity only.

8–10 · high

Cinematic

Scroll-driven reveals, parallax, continuous loops. Must be motivated and reduced-motion safe.

Density · 信息密度 — art gallery to cockpit

1–3 · low

Art gallery

Huge whitespace, big type, section gaps py-32+. Feels expensive and clean.

4–7 · mid

Daily app

Standard web spacing. The comfortable default for most product and marketing pages.

8–10 · high

Cockpit

Tight padding, cards become 1px divider lines, mono numbers for aligned data.

Redesign rule of thumb: for "preserve" match the existing dials and add +1 motion; for "overhaul" push variance and motion +2 while keeping the existing density.
